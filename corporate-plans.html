Frequently Asked Questions

What happens when an employee completes their course? ▼
Their seat becomes "Alumni" status and is automatically freed. You can immediately add a new employee to that seat at no extra cost.
What if an employee fails the course? ▼
They get 1 free retry. If they fail again, the seat goes inactive. You can either purchase a retry ($2/seat) or replace them with a new employee.
Can I add more seats mid-year? ▼
Yes! You can top-up additional seats at $12/seat anytime. Business plan users get priority access to seat top-ups.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept USDT (TRC-20) cryptocurrency payments. This ensures fast, global transactions with no currency conversion fees.
What courses are available for corporate teams? ▼
Python, AI for Business, Leadership & Management, Finance & Accounting, Blockchain & Web3, and more. Our AI can also generate custom courses based on your team's specific needs.
How does batch course assignment work? ▼
In your corporate dashboard, select multiple employees and assign them the same course with one click. All their progress is tracked together in a batch report.
